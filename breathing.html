BREATHING

Injury

1. Open airway using jaw thrust and prepare suction
2. CONSIDER Plastic Airway (Give 15 litres/min and Oxygen via free flow mask)
3. Treat traumatic injuries and Keep warm with a blanket
4. Monitor and evacuate
5. Open airway using head tilt chin lift and prepare suction

Illness/Immersion

1. Open airway using head tilt chin lift and prepare suction
2. CONSIDER Plastic Airway (Give 15 litres/min and Oxygen via free flow mask)
3. Place in recovery, position and keep warm in blanket
4. Monitor and evacuate
