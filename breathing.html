BREATHING

Injury

Open airway using jaw thrust and prepare suction CONSIDER- Plastic Airway (Give 15 litres/min and Oxygen via free flow mask)

Treat traumatic injuries and Keep warm with a blanket

Monitor and evacuate

Illness/Immersion

Open airway using head tilt chin lift and prepare suction CONSIDER- Plastic Airway (Give 15 litres/min and Oxygen via free flow mask)

Place in recovery, position and keep warm in blanket

Monitor and evacuate
